Hello!

I am currently a Fulbright Chile Scholar, working with Prof. Jose Correa at the Universidad de Chile in research in mechanism design and applications in transit networks. I recently completed my masters in Computer Science at MIT advised by Prof. Yael Kalai, with my thesis focused on lattice-based cryptography.

In my free time, I like photography, planespotting, running, cycling, and learning languages. I’ve lived and/or worked in USA, Hong Kong, Japan, China, Germany, and Chile so far.
