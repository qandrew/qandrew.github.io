Hello friends! I am a MIT 2017 majoring in Math and Computer Science (18C). This summer, I am in Berlin, Germany interning at Pupil Labs.

While at MIT, I am the director of program evaluation for Amphibious Achievement, the outgoing vice president of the Pi Lambda Phi Fraternity, and the secretary of the Undergraduate Math Association .

Please feel free to explore this website! I hope to show you who I am as a student, and also as a person with diverse interests.
