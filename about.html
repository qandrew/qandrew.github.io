Hello, world! I am a Chinese-American member of the world, Massachusetts Institute of Technology Class of 2017, currently studying math and computer science (18C in tech jargon).

An international background has been in me since my coming into the world. Born in New York to Chinese parents, I spent twelve years combined living in Hong Kong and Tokyo, before moving back to Boston for high school and college. I have lived in five countries1(America, Hong Kong, China, Japan, Germany), and am desiring to travel to many more. I am fluent in English and Chinese and am working my way through learning Japanese and German.

Traveling is one of my passions, and I have coupled my interest in photography whilst on adventures. In my spare time, I enjoy reading books, watching movies, spending time with friends, and thinking about the meaning of life (trust me, I am quite clueless about where I want to head). Please feel free to explore this website and get a glimpse into my life.
