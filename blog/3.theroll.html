How is my mental state when I am stressed?

March 20, 2014

That cultural change may be the harder one yet the one requiring less technical knowledge, I understand. That in our fiery essence, in the heart of the soul, in the nonconvention in symphony with the convention, that we continue to beat up and on and iterate into time and into time more, I know. That I'mma keep on smoking 'til I can't hit another note, I commit. That I am me and I am not me, I stand true. That I have 802 to study for, I request.

But with all of this mixing pot, I iterate and iterate. I am a roll and I continue to roll. 2014 come and get me. It is getting me, and I'm glad that the spring break is coming up. I have no clue what to expect out of spring break, and I have no clue of what to expect out of my life. But there are things that keep beating me -beating me onwards. Bluegreyblue eyes, this evernote, music, the unfinished Atlas Shrugged, Hongyi's mediocrity and in myself included, and the undying Korean Bitch. What have I lost, and what keeps me pure, what keeps me inundated, what keeps me me, if I am still me.

While we live in four dimensions -time trapping us and carrying into the future, into the future, into the future we go -we are in fact three-dimensional objects. While we do iterate onwards in time, the present is a three-dimensional entity, that I am me in March 2014, that the March 2014 me is different from the 2013 me, the 2010 me, that we ebb and flow. In discrete movement or in continuum I do not know, I have no knowledge of, but I am me and this text can complete an incomplete capture.

Is language inherently a limitation or an expander, as all of our vocabulary is a subset of the language meta, that if we can not express what is out, then is our culture the fundamental limitation?

What does it mean to be a human? What part of me is me? Is it just my physical body? Does it include my past, my present, and my future? What about my qualities, my internal and external attributes? Does everyone have an internal, or are we just amalgamations of influences on society? Is the external comprised of the physical or the meta-physical -the clothing we wear, the food we eat, the community we are in, or more: our inter-network, our connections and lines and dots -or will we ever know?

Kai is impressive.

What is music, especially when I zone out and I can't complete the work that I do. Is it just a series of frequencies and waves, that work in conjunction when you are in the mood? And when you zone out it's just the noise? Selena and the Noise? Marina and the Noise? 2 Andrew Xia in the noise?

What is my name and what does it mean? Where did the sound and the symbolism come from, the Andrew that I am referred to?

What is my culture? Am I out of the meta, can I truly step out? Should I have a Chinese name like Hongyi (what does that even mean) or be like Jack Ma and use nicknames, for further protection? Why don't the Japanese, the Korean, the Indian conform as much?

What do I want to do and what will my GPA come out as?

Is the world losing blue eyes, and in the case that I can't, I can't, then what am I?

And how do I expend the gender meta, if that is something that I care about?

What is a person, do I like people, who do I like, why do I like her if I do, what is her1 or her2 or her3 or her4 or relics of a past that I can't stop won't stop going back to?

What is a hashtag?

What is conformity?

What is this language, a series of characters, that I ride on?

And in the establishment of time as a driver, as a screwdriver, as a screw, where do I iterate onwards to? I do sometimes look forward, that the expansion of Andrew into newer areas -at the cost of contractions that I long, long forgot -at its surface always seems beautiful, pristine, meaningful, but it's a tradeoff. Pilam for East Campus. 777 for 747. Wife for parents. Sex for innocence. The future for the past, but never, never, the other way around. Never. Never. Nie. So moreso it is a tradeoff that I have no leverage on.

Do I still have the attention span to read a book? Is my restless heart going to rest? At least right now I can write.

But do, please do get me with those green eyes baby as the lights come down. April 4th, 2014, inside mediocrity, I present myself the opportunity of the symbolism of her. Tomorrow I target.

What is wrong with my mind?

Iterating currently in the meta, in the depths beyond depths, in areas that I am still fundamentally confused about. Do not go here, they say, do not ask those questions. Is my approach too narrow, am I too different from the world? JA783A stares at me (actually right now it's the red hair and the eyest eyes of Marina and the Diamonds). Iterate me. Iterate me more. Ho ho, the me-est me and the Andrewest Andrew and the iteration of language, god out of the well bring me more, bring me more.

Suicide is out of the question, it's a cheap escape, but it's that wild card. Quentin in The Sound and the Fury takes the easy path. I would like to iterate more. Of course right now I see a sea of purposelessness, a plethora of selfish second-handers at MIT, that it is the I first mindset. But I am the eye, the best me in the world, the one that is a bit too different. Unfortunately. Do I stay pure? Is this canvas of mine trapped in the metas of this journal, this writing that does not comprehend and does not compress?

But as he says, into time as you head will you settle down.

Do I like the way I am right now? Into spring break I head, half of my spring term is over, yet it still feels like it just began. I have an 802 test to compete in. I would like an A in that class. Hopefully in 5111, 21f402, and 15411 too. But it's not the end of the world. It's not the end of the world.

Because this world is mine, and when I decide to commit and when I decide to be able to commit hopefully I'll create something. I'm not like the Harvard where I have a vision but no skill. I'm not like the MIT where I'm a machine where my skill skyrockets, but I have no individual mind. I am me, Andrew fucking Xia, on March 20th, 2014.
