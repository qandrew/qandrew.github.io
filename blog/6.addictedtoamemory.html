Addicted to a Memory

Hello World, China

September 10, 2015

Beijing, China: Hello World. Ten days into

What is my goal here? I know my life back in Boston awaits for my return, and that my comfort zone and community will still be there.
As for my time here at Peking University, I have three main goals. Firstly, I want to immerse myself in Chinese culture, and improve my Chinese so I can fully blend in. I hope to improve my Chinese to a point such that others won't be able to tell I did not grow up outside of the country. Secondly, I want to meet other Chinese people my age, and make strong friendships that will be strong. Though I have relatives in China, I do not know many people my age. Finally, with time away from the Institute, I'd also like this fall to be a cathartic and transcending experience, as I try to decide how I want to spend my last two years in college, instead of simply conforming and following the herd.
